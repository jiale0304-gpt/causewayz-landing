Why Causewayz Square

Built where two economies meet

Covered link bridge to CIQ & RTS

A fully sheltered, barrier-free walkway connects the retail podium directly to the CIQ complex and the future RTS station — a safe, comfortable commute in any weather.

  • Walk to JB Sentral & Komtar JBCC
  • Reach Woodlands in ~15 min via CIQ today
  • Just 5 min to Singapore once RTS opens (Dec 2026)

Retail convenience at your doorstep

Approximately 90,000 sqft of curated retail brings dining, wellness and daily essentials a lift ride away — designed to serve residents first, and the growing flow of cross-border visitors.

  • 40 retail units within the development
  • Minutes from JBCC malls, hospitals & universities
  • Strong footfall from CIQ & RTS commuters

Resort-calibre facilities in the sky

From infinity pools to co-working spaces, the lifestyle podium is designed for how residents actually live — unwinding after the cross-border commute or working from home in style.

  • Infinity & horizon pools, sky jacuzzi
  • Gym, yoga deck, games & karaoke rooms
  • Co-working space, playground, sky garden

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Causewayz Square freehold?
Yes. Causewayz Square is a freehold mixed development in Johor Bahru City Centre — ownership has no expiry period, making it attractive for long-term holding and investment.
Can foreigners purchase a unit?
Yes, foreigners are eligible to purchase, subject to the prevailing state minimum purchase price requirements. Contact us for the latest eligibility details.
Where exactly is the project located?
Jalan Lumba Kuda, Johor Bahru — about 600m from the CIQ checkpoint, with a covered link bridge connecting to CIQ and the future RTS Bukit Chagar station.
When is the expected completion?
The current phase is expected to complete around Q4 2029 – 2031 depending on tower. Register your interest and we'll share the latest timeline for each tower.
How much is the maintenance fee?
Estimated RM0.36 per sq ft per month, inclusive of sinking fund.
Is there a showroom I can visit?
Yes — the show unit is open daily by appointment. WhatsApp us to reserve your viewing slot.
